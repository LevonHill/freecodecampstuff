Yummy Sweet Potatoes

Hello, and greatings to one of the most valuable cooking guides! Today we are going to learn how to make Sweat potatoes better known as 'Yams'. Follow this step by step guide to make a sweet and tasty ,yet healthy vegetable.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 medium sweet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • optinal: 1/2 teaspoon paprika or cinnamon (for extra flavor)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Wash, peel, and chop sweet potatoes into even-sized cubes (about 1 inch).
  2. Toss the cubes in olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and any optional spices. Coat them evenly.
  3. Arrange the cubes on a baking sheet — single layer, no overlapping. Give them space to crisp, not steam.
  4. Bake for 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway. You want golden edges and fork-tender centers.
  5. Remove from the oven. Let them rest 2–3 minutes. Then eat — alone, or as a side to your next conquest.
